# Apparel-Industry-Project-US-Project-
US Based Bookkeeping project in QBO for a 3-store apparel business in CA, covering Jan–Mar 2025. Included full company setup, COA, products/services, multi-location tracking, 2 bank accounts, 1 credit card, bills, sales, payroll, loans, depreciation, and adjusting entries.

Comprehensive bookkeeping project for a 3-store apparel business across San Francisco, Los Angeles, and San Diego. Covers Jan–Mar 2025 with full company setup, COA, products/services, location & class tracking, detailed bank and credit card reconciliations, bills, sales, payroll, loans, depreciation, prepaid accounts, and adjusting entries to ensure accurate financial reporting.

**Key Tasks Completed

Set up company profile, Chart of Accounts, and product/service list

Applied Location and Class tracking for all three stores

Processed 2 bank statements (224 & 290 transactions)

Entered and reconciled 1 credit card account (91 transactions)

Recorded 49 vendor bills and 280 sales entries

Managed payroll and related expenses

Posted adjusting entries, depreciation, prepaid insurance, and advance rent

Handled long-term and short-term loan accounts

Ensured accurate reporting for Jan 1 – Mar 31, 2025
